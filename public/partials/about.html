About the App

XCursion is a web application that allows you to track the places you've been to and the places you want to go to. After creating an account, users have the ability to add places to their "Want To" and "Been To" lists, or even copy other user's places onto one of their two lists.

Other app features include searching through all public posts in the Home page, editing and deleting any of your created posts, and adding images and descriptions to your posts.

About the Team

This app was brought to you by a team of passionate developers and travel afficionados with the hopes of providing users an easy way to share and track their excursions. The developers behind this app consisted of three students from General Assembly's Web Development Immersive Remote course. As the course name implies, all three of them worked on this from different parts of the country. After several video chats later, a couple thousand lines of code and several GitHub commits, they've made XCursion available for users on a desktop and mobile browser.
